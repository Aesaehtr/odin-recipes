Naan

The famous Indian bread

Learn to make this amazing bread at home

It's easy and delicious

Ingredients

  • flour
  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Make the dough
  3. let it rest for 2 hours
  4. Divide into small balls
  5. Flatten and cook for 1 to 2 minutes per side
  6. Garnish and enjoy
